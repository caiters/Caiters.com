About Caitlin

I've been making websites ever since I was in high school. Back then, I didn't know too much about HTML and had no clue was CSS was, but it didn't stop me from trying. I made a fansite for my favorite anime, Evangelion, and since then I haven't stopped!

In the years since, I've learned a lot more about making websites, but the main thing that still sticks with me is it's a lot of fun to make them! I love what I do.

My other big hobbies are video games (I play just about everything), reading, writing, and drawing. When I'm not doing one of those, I can usually be found playing with one of my critters or with my daughter!
